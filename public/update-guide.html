Update CANA

Keep this browser tab open — it stays with you while CANA closes and the new version takes its place.

Download the update Preparing your download…

Couldn't find a direct download link. Open the releases page and download the .dmg manually.

Your saved data is not touched by updating — it stays on your Mac. You won't lose anything.
  1. 1

    Wait for the download to finish

    Look for the .dmg file in your Downloads. It's a one-time ~100–150 MB download.

  2. 2

    Open the downloaded .dmg

    Double-click it. A window opens showing the CANA app and an Applications shortcut.

  3. 3

    Quit the current CANA

    Switch to CANA and press ⌘ Q (or CANA → Quit). This tab stays open — it's in your browser, not the app, so it can't be closed by quitting CANA.

  4. 4

    Drag CANA into Applications

    In the .dmg window, drag the CANA icon onto the Applications folder. When macOS asks, choose Replace to overwrite the old version.

  5. 5

    Open the new CANA

    Launch CANA from Applications. The first time, macOS may say it's from an unidentified developer — right-click the app, choose Open, then Open again. Your data and the new version will be there.

Tip: you can eject the .dmg afterward (drag it to the Trash / eject in Finder). The "unidentified developer" step only happens because the app isn't yet code-signed; it's normal and safe for an app you built yourself.
